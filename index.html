VisioSense: Smart AI Glasses for the Visually Impaired

Role: Technical Project Team Lead
AI & Computer Vision

Built on the foundational belief that everyone deserves equal accessibility and autonomy in daily life, VisioSense is a comprehensive assistive wearable designed to accurately map the parameters of physical environments for visually impaired and partially sighted individuals. Built and tested across 4 core engineering phases:

01 Real-time Object Detection
Identifies complex environmental obstacles and instantly relays descriptive directional audio alerts to warn users of oncoming layout hazards.
02 Color & Currency Recognition
Deciphers localized environments—ranging from tracking traffic signal adjustments to actively verifying and calculating physical currency denominations.
03 Micro-Navigation Assistance
Unlike standard consumer GPS frameworks that recalculate maps automatically, VisioSense alerts users the millisecond they diverge from safe paths.
04 Smart Transit & Bus Tracking
Instantly reads approaching transit vehicle identification markers and announces destination routes and exact arrival directional bearings.
